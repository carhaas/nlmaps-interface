NLmaps

About

OpenStreetMap (OSM) is a community-built database of geographic data, containing user contributed local and up-to-date information about landmarks all over the world. Our tool, NLmaps, enables a sophisticated search of the database via an interface that supports questions phrased in natural language.


Licence Notes

The OSM related data is available under the Open Database Licence

A semantic parser (available under the Apache License 2.0) based on Statistical Machine Translation (SMT) and the NLmaps v2 corpus (available under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International) were used to create this tool. The released corpus consists of 28,609 questions, each with a corresponding Machine Readable Formula (MRL) with which the correct answer can be found if executed against the OSM database.

The NLmaps backend (available under the Affero GPL v3) may be used to obtain an answer to a corresponding MRL formula. It is based on based on the Overpass-API (also available under the Affero GPL v3).
